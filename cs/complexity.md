Favourite Proofs in Complexity

* NP ≠ SPACE (n)
	- Both NP ⊂ SPACE (n) and SPACE (n) ⊂  NP are open problems. Yet we know NP ≠ SPACE (n). How? 
		- *closure under logspace reductions.* - NP is closed, while SPACE (n) is not.
